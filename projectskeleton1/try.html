እውነት ነገር ፣ ጽድቅ ነገር

Titled Yezembilu Tikusat or The Headache of the Basket. Going from age 18-21, the time spent in college can be entirely disorienting. Maturing into adulthood while finding myself so far from home and far from everything I had identified with up until then, there is a need imposed to quickly establish who I am or get swept away by the world, disregarded. In the poem, the basket resembles a container that each individual possesses. In this container is stored an individuals’ unique gifts and capabilities. It’s not easy to choose to carry the basket and to choose what inside it over what’s outside it but which one will allow us to feel most centered? Perhaps that answer is apparent.
